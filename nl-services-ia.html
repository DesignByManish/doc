Noel Leeming Services IA

Noel Leeming offers a great range of services to help customers with their latest tech purchase, and one of the business goals was to increase the attach rate of services with products purchased online. We started design discovery by reviewing the current experience to understand user needs and pain points. Based on this we created an experience strategy and roadmap (read the case study), where we identified awareness and discoverability as one of the key themes to focus on.

Problem statement

How might we make our services offering discoverable for customers visiting the website to drive awareness?

We started with focusing on the foundation – the information architecture of how Noel Leeming Services were structured in the navigation. Being an important starting point for user discovery, it was crucial to create the right hierarchy to make the range of services visible to the users.

Problems with old IA

  • Unrelated sections nested under Services makes it difficult to emphasise on the range of services we offer, making it less discoverable.
  • Incorrect naming conventions and inconsistencies, which may not be what user’s normally relate to.

    • Process

    We conducted a tree test on old IA against proposed IA to understand which was closer to users’ mental model.

    We created a set of tasks for users to find specific information basing it on our hypotheses.

    The tree test helped us get insights on our hypothesis and our users’ mental model.

    The overall results helped in validating that the new IA was closer to users’ mental model and expectations, with an increase in success rate by 18%. Although it still wasn’t a great success rate, this test helped in highlighting the risks of using business terminology for few services.

    Based on the results and insights, we updated the IA to make it easier for users to discover the range of services.

    Results

    Updating the IA proved to be great success with an overall 113% growth in page views across all devices helping in improved discoverability and eventually awareness of our services range.
